GCP will enable cities to create their own sustainable initiatives or use existing apps to help them fulfill Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

GCP - Green City Protocol is a set of tools, instructions and materials to help each city set up an advanced IT platform that can host specialized apps for rewarding sustainable behavior of their citizens and tourists in their city. The city will have its own website and mobile platform using a unique payment system. The platform will inform citizens about events that include "green" actions that they can participate and contribute to. Each platform will host specific applications that will reward citizens with the tokens they can use for various local services, from paying cultural events to payments for municipal services.
